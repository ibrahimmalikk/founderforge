# FounderForge

FounderForge is an AI startup simulator where a player creates a startup, makes weekly business decisions, and tries to survive for 12 simulated weeks.

The goal is to build a simple, fun, and slightly dramatic game that helps players experience the pressure of running an early-stage startup.

## Core Game Loop

1. Create a startup
2. View starting stats
3. Choose a weekly business decision
4. See the startup’s stats change
5. Read a short story about what happened
6. Move to the next week
7. Try to survive 12 weeks

## Starting Stats

The game currently tracks:

- Cash
- Users
- Revenue
- Product Quality
- Reputation

## Example Startup

Name: DormDash  
Industry: Campus Services  
Product: Late-night snack delivery for college students  
Target Customer: Students living in dorms

## Example Decision

Decision: Spend $5,000 on marketing

Possible result:

- Cash decreases
- Users increase
- Revenue increases slightly
- Reputation increases
- Product Quality stays the same

## First Version Goal

The first version of FounderForge should let a player:

- Create a startup
- See starting stats
- Make one decision per week
- See stats change
- Continue until Week 12

No multiplayer, payments, real investors, or complicated features yet.
